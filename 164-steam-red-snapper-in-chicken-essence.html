Steam Red Snapper in Chicken Essence instructions

  1. Wash fish clean with water and butterfly cut the fish..
  2. Blanch the fish in hot boiling water for 30 seconds to remove fishy smell..
  3. Place spring onion and ginger on steaming plate. Place fish on top. Add in the mixture of seasoning.
  4. Steam the fish for 10 minutes in high heat. Remove fish into a serving plate. Add in the sauce from the steaming plate..
  5. Fry the garlic and ginger with 2 tbsp oil till brown. Add in Shaoxing wine and 1 tbsp soy sauce for aroma. Garnish fish with spring onions slices. Pour in the hot oil with the fried garlic and ginger on top of fish. Ready to serve hot..
