Ingredients of Mintymama Club Sandwich

  1. Prepare 4 slices of Sandwich bread.
  2. It's 6 slices of Salted ox tongue.
  3. It's 2 slices of Swiss cheese.
  4. Prepare 2 of Large eggs (scrambled).
  5. You need 4 slices of Lettuce.
  6. Prepare 1 of Tomato (sliced).
  7. It's 6 pieces of Mushroom (sliced).
  8. Prepare of Homemade Sandwich Sauce.
  9. It's 2 tsp of Ketcup.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of Mayonnaise.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Mustard.
  12. It's of Salt and pepper.

Mintymama Club Sandwich step by step

  1. Scramble the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Sautée the mushrooms with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix & spread the Mintymama Sandwich Sauce on side of the bread.
  4. Assemble & layer all the toppings on the bread.
  5. Prepare a piece of parchment paper and wrap the sandwich tightly.
  6. Use a sharp bread knife to cut the sandwich into halves and serve.
