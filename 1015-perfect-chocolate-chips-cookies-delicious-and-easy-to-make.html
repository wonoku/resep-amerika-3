Ingredients of perfect chocolate chips cookies

  1. You need 2 stick of butter.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup of white sugar.
  4. It's 1 of egg.
  5. You need 1 of egg yolk.
  6. You need 2 tsp of good vanilla extract.
  7. It's 1 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  8. You need 2 1/4 cup of flour.
  9. Prepare 1 pinch of salt.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of chocolate chips of your choice.

perfect chocolate chips cookies instructions

  1. preheat oven to 345.
  2. soften butter but not quite to liquid.
  3. cream together butter and both brown and white sugar until smooth.
  4. add vanilla, egg and egg yolk continue stiring until smooth.
  5. in seperate bowl combine flour baking soda and salt.
  6. slowly fold dry ingrediants into butter mixture until a nice dough forms.
  7. fold in chocolate chips.
  8. form one tablespoon balls onto non greased cookie sheet and bake 10-12 minutes depending how chewy or crispy you like them.
