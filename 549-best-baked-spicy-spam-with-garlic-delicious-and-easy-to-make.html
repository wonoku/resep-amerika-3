Ingredients of Baked Spicy Spam With Garlic

  1. It's 1 can of spam any flavour.
  2. Prepare 3 of deseeded red chili pepper or any pepper optional.
  3. It's 4 clove of pounded or dice garlic.
  4. It's of GARNISH.
  5. You need 3 tbsp of spring onion / scallions optional.

Baked Spicy Spam With Garlic instructions

  1. mash spam with fork and top with pounded garlic and red chili pepper.
  2. bake at 200 C for 15 minute then stir it and bake for another 15 minute.
  3. serve bake spicy spam top spring onion / scallions on dinner rolls top parmesan cheese.
