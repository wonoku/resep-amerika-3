Spam and hashbrown casserole step by step

  1. Cook hashbrowns according to package. Season to taste..
  2. In separate pot combine cream of mushroom, sour cream, splash of milk, seasonings, and cheese. Heat through and stir until mixed well..
  3. Remove.spam from can And.chop into bite sized pieces. Add to hashbrowns then add soup mixture And cabbage and mix well..
  4. Put in oven and heat through mixing every so often. Next add crushed crackers and drizzle melted butter on top..
  5. Place in oven and bake for about 5-10 minutes. Remove from oven serve and enjoy!! Be careful, it's very hot!!.
