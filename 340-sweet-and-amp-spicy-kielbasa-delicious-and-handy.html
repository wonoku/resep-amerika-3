Ingredients of Sweet & Spicy Kielbasa

  1. It's 1 tbsp of garlic.
  2. Prepare 1 dash of salt.
  3. It's 1 dash of pepper.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of sugar.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of parsley.
  6. Prepare 1 of medium purple onion.
  7. You need 1 of medium jalapeno.
  8. You need 1 of medium lime.
  9. Prepare 2 1/2 of lbs salt.

Sweet & Spicy Kielbasa step by step

  1. Slice sausage in .25 inch pieces at an angle..
  2. Put sausage in large pan over medium heat..
  3. Cut onion into medium pieces..
  4. Slice jalapeno into thin slices..
  5. Add onion and jalapeno to sausage..
  6. Squeeze the juice of lime into pan..
  7. Add garlic, sugar, salt, pepper, and parsley..
  8. Cover and simmer over medium heat 20 minutes or until sausage is done. Stir occassionally..
