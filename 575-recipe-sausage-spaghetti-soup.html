Ingredients of Sausage Spaghetti Soup

  1. It's 1 Tbsp of butter.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of onion, minced.
  3. It's 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  4. It's 3 pcs of beef sausages, cut 1 cm.
  5. Prepare 100 gr of spam, cut rectangle.
  6. Prepare 1 pc of boneless chicken thigh, boiled, diced.
  7. It's 2 of carrots, peeled, cut.
  8. Prepare 100 gr of frozen peas.
  9. You need 2 of tomatoes, cut into 8 wedges.
  10. It's 1500 ml of chicken stock.
  11. It's 100 gr of spaghetti, boiled al dente according to the package directions, set aside.
  12. You need 2 tsp of salt or to taste.
  13. You need 1/2 tsp of white pepper powder.
  14. It's 1/2 tsp of mushroom bouillon.
  15. You need 3/4 tsp of nutmeg powder.
