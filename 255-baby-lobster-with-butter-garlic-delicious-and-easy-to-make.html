Cooking Tips

The garlic cooks quickly in 20 seconds (perhaps less as it is dependent upon how popular the pan is). If the garlic is cooked too long, then it is going to taste not quite pleasant and lose its aroma. If you would like to add garlic, make certain you add it last, so it doesn't burn.

Ingredients of Baby Lobster with Butter Garlic

  1. It's 8 of baby lobster.
  2. It's 8 tsp of Chopped Garlic.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of Salted Butter.
  4. Prepare Pinch of Salt.
  5. It's Pinch of Suger.
  6. You need 2 tsp of Lime Juice.

Baby Lobster with Butter Garlic step by step

  1. Cleaning the lobster and boil with hot water for about 15min.
  2. Add in cooking oil and Stir-fried 4tbsp of garlic until brownish, drain out the cooking oil and mixed with the other 4tbsp of raw garlic..
  3. Raw & cooked garlic mixed well then add in salted butter and lime juice and stirring well and ready to be servrf76.
