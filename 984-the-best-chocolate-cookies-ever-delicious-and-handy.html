The best chocolate cookies ever!!! instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mix oil, sugar, vanilla, and melted chocolate (you can use a spoon).
  2. Stir in eggs (one at a time), flour, and baking powder..
  3. Add salt.
  4. Let dough chill for at least 3 hours or over night.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  6. Place chilled dough on a GREASED cookie sheet (the balls should be about 1 inch and they should be 1 inch apart).
  7. Roll each ball in the powdered sugar.
  8. Cook in the oven for 10-11 minutes.
  9. Immediately place the cooked cookies onto a wire rack and let cool.
  10. (Your cookies will be puffy and still sort of shaped like balls. Do not flatten!! They will sink themselves when cooled).
  11. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
  12. Store in an airtight GLASS container (they will Keep for 4 days).
  13. Enjoy!!.
