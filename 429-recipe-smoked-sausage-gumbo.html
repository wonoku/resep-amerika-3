Ingredients of Smoked Sausage Gumbo

  1. Prepare 2 of celery ribs, chopped.
  2. Prepare 1 medium of onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 medium of green or red pepper, chopped.
  4. It's 1 medium of carrot, chopped.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  7. It's 1 cup of chicken broth.
  8. Prepare 1 lb of smoked kielbasa or Polish sausage, cut into 1/2-in. pieces.
  9. Prepare 1 can of diced tomatoes, undrained.
  10. It's 2 tsp of dried oregano.
  11. Prepare 2 tsp of dried thyme.

Smoked Sausage Gumbo step by step

  1. In a large skillet, saute celery, onion, green/red pepper, and carrot in oil until tender..
  2. Stir in flour until blended; gradually add broth. Bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened..
  3. Transfer to a slow cooker. Stir in the sausage, tomatoes, oregano, thyme and cayenne..
  4. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until heated through. (gumbo will seem thick at first but will thin will cooking).
  5. Serve over rice..
