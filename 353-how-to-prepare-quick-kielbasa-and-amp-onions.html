Ingredients of Quick Kielbasa & Onions

  1. You need 1 lb of kielbasa, sliced.
  2. You need 2 medium of Onions, sliced.
  3. It's 1/2 of green bell pepper, diced.
  4. It's 2 clove of garlic, minced.
  5. It's 1 dash of fennel seed.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of celery salt.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of dill weed.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of oil.

Quick Kielbasa & Onions step by step

  1. Heat oil in large sauce pan and add all ingredients except kielbasa and dill..
  2. Sauté over med/high heat till onions are translucent ~7mins.
  3. Add kielbasa and sauté over medium heat another ~7mins.
  4. Stir in dill weed for last 1-2 mins of cooking.
  5. I like to serve this with pierogi's and a dill dipping sauce ( 1/2c sour cream, 1 1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tsp dill).
