  6. You need 1/2 cup of hazelnuts.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of goji berries.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of black cherries, quartered and pitted.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of coconut flakes.
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp of agave syrup.
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp of virgin coconut oil.
  12. You need 1/2 tsp of pure vanilla extract.

Super healthy vegan granola instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, ensuring that the ingredients are well mixed. Spread thinly on baking sheets.
  2. Bake in the oven at 300F for about 10-15 minutes, ensuring that you don't burn it..
  3. Serve with vegan almond milk and banana slices, or enjoy as is..
