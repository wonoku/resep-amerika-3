Ingredients of Easy delicious pot roast

  2. Prepare 1 of unseasoned meat tenderizer.
  3. Prepare 1 of garlic powder.
  4. Prepare 1 of dry ranch seasoning.
  5. You need 1 of ground black pepper.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of brown gravy.
  7. Prepare 2 large of carrots peeled and chopped.
  8. It's 1 medium of sweet onion.
  9. Prepare 1 medium of red potato chopped.
  10. It's 1 medium of russet potato chopped.

Easy delicious pot roast instructions

  1. Put roast in crock pot and sprinkle tenderizer on all sides. Season with the garlic powder, ranch seasoning, and pepper..
  2. Rinse your veggies then peel/ chop the carrots. Next chop both potatoes and the onion. Place veggies all around the roast in the crock pot..
  3. Mix up your gravy and pour over meat and veggies. Put lid on and cook on high for about 6 hours. ****( I flipped mine over halfway through cooking time and added Extra seasonings.)
