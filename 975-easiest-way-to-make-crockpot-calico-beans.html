Ingredients of Crockpot Calico Beans

  1. Prepare 1 lb. of hamburger.
  2. You need 1 of small onion, chopped.
  3. It's 2 cans of pork and beans.
  4. It's 1 can of great northern beans, drained.
  5. You need 1 can of pinto beans, drained.
  6. You need 1 can of kidney beans, drained.
  7. It's 10-12 slices of bacon, cooked & crumbled.
  8. You need 1 cup of ketchup.
  9. Prepare 1 cup of brown sugar.
  10. Prepare 2 Tbsp. of white vinegar.
  11. Prepare of Few dashes of your favorite hot sauce.
  12. You need of Seasonings of choice:.
  13. Prepare of pepper, chili powder, garlic powder, cumin.

Crockpot Calico Beans step by step

  1. Brown hamburger along with onions and seasonings of choice in skillet until thoroughly cooked; drain..
  2. Open and drain cans of beans with exception of pork and beans..
  3. Place cooked hamburger in crockpot..
  4. Add beans..
  5. Add cooked, crumbled bacon..
  6. Add ketchup and brown sugar..
  7. Mix well to combine. Cook in crockpot for 4 hours on low..
  8. 30 minutes before serving, add in white vinegar and hot sauce (as many dashes as you like). I did about 6 shakes..
  9. Enjoy along with some cornbread if your budget allows! Cornbread is another fantastic cheap side!.
