Ingredients of Rice+cauliflower+sweet corn+egg yolk+cheese baby porridge

  2. You need of Few small chunks of cauliflower.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of sweet corn puree.
  4. You need 1 of boiled egg yolk.
  5. It's 400 ml of homemade chicken stock.
  6. It's 1 block of plain cheese(laughing cow brand).

Rice+cauliflower+sweet corn+egg yolk+cheese baby porridge instructions

  2. Add chicken stock and cauliflower.
  3. Set to auto mode estimated for around 3 hours.
  4. When become porridge, add sweet corn puree and egg yolk. Stir well.
  5. Add grated cheese on top before serving.
