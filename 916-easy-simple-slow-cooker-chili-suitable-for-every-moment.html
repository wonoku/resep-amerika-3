Easy Simple Slow-Cooker Chili step by step

  1. Preheat the slow-cooker.
  2. Brown meats & drain off fat; add to slow-cooker..
  3. Add all can ingredients to slow-cooker..
  4. Saute' onions & green peppers in olive oil for 3 minutes; add to slow-cooker..
  5. Set slow-cooker for 6 hours and let it cook; when done; ready to serve..
  6. Note: "If you would like thicker chili; just mash or add to a blender 1 to 2 cups of beans; then add to the chili.".
