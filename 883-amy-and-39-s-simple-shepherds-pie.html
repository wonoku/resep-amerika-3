Ingredients of Amy's Simple Shepherds Pie

  1. You need 2 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 of 16oz. jar of beef gravy.
  3. Prepare 1 large of onion, diced.
  4. It's 1 box of sour cream & onion instant mashed potatoes.
  5. It's 1 box of cheese flavored instant mashed potatoes.
  6. Prepare 1 of 12oz. package of shredded cheddar cheese.
  7. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of season salt.
  8. You need 3 large of eggs, beatened.

Amy's Simple Shepherds Pie instructions

  1. Brown ground beef & season with season salt or to taste. Add onion 3/4 way cooking. Drain grease, add gravy & set aside..
  2. Prepare potatoes as directed on package. Mixing all packages together. When potatoes almost done, add 1/2 package of shredded cheese..
  3. In 13x9 in. pan, put meat on bottom & spread. Then add potatoes on top & spread..
  4. Take eggs & pour over top of potatoes. Poke holes in potatoes to get egg to help combine dish..
  5. Sprinkle rest of cheese on top, cover with foil & bake for 30 minutes in a 375Â° preheated oven..
  6. Serve with a side of veggies. I use frozen mixed veggies, peas, corn, or carrots..
