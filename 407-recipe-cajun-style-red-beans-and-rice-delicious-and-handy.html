Ingredients of Cajun style red beans and rice

  1. It's 1 box of family size Zatarain's red beans and rice.
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of butter or vegetable oil.
  3. You need 2 large of green bellpeppers.
  4. Prepare 1 medium of onion.
  5. You need 2 packages of Polska Kielbasa OR Andoullie sausage*.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of Cajun seasoning.
  8. It's 1 packages of Gouda cheese (or preferred cheese, Cheddar also goes well).

Cajun style red beans and rice step by step

  1. Chop (dice) your bellpeppers and onion and slice sausage and set aside..
  2. In a large saucepan or pot add all ingredients for the red beans and rice (beans and rice, water and oil or butter)..
  3. Add sausage, bellpeppers, onion, garlic and Cajun seasoning and cook according to package directions..
  4. Cut the Gouda (or preferred cheese) into small cubes and add about five minutes before cooking is complete, or in the last five minutes that it sits..
  5. Serve and enjoy!.
