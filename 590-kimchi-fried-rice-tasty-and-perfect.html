Kimchi fried rice step by step

  1. Cook white rice.
  2. Dice spam into cubes and slice kimchi into small pieces (dice garlic too if using).
  3. Fry spam in pan on medium high to high heat (with the diced garlic) remove from heat.
  4. Fry slices kimchi pieces for about 45 seconds on medium heat.
  5. Add white rice to pot and stir in kimchi and spam.
  6. Add kimchi juice and mix well until no white can be seen.
  7. Mix in sesame oil to taste and Allow to cook for about 2-5 min on medium.
  8. Top with green onions and serve.
