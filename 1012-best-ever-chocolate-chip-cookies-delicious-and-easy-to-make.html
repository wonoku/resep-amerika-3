Best ever chocolate chip cookies step by step

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside..
  2. In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, belt the butter, stirring constantly until it reaches a golden brown color; remove from heat immediately..
  3. Transfer melted butter to a large mixing bowl. Add sugars and vanilla; beat until light and fluffy: about 3 minutes. Add egg and stir..
  4. Add the dry ingredients, small amounts at a time, until combined..
  5. Fold in chocolate chunks..
  6. Use a 2 tablespoon measuring spoon to scoop out the dough and place them on a prepared baking sheet..

  8. Serve with milk!.
