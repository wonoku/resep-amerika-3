Ingredients of Red snapper fish fry

  1. It's 500 g of fish (red snapper/sankara).

  4. You need 1/2 tsp of garam masala powder.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of ginger garlic paste.
  6. Prepare To taste of salt.

Red snapper fish fry instructions

  1. Clean the fish pieces well. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and garam masala powder..
  2. Next add salt and ginger garlic paste. Mix everything till all the pieces are coated..
  3. You can shallow fry in tawa or deep fry in oil too. This is tawa fry. Flip and cook both sides until done..
