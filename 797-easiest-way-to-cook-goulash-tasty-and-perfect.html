Ingredients of Goulash

  1. Prepare 2.5 lbs of lean ground beef.
  2. Prepare 2 of small onions chopped.
  3. You need 2/3 of red and yellow pepper chopped.
  4. It's 1 &1/2 Tbs of Minced garlic.
  5. Prepare 1 can of beef broth.
  6. You need 6 ounce of tomato paste.
  7. Prepare 1 quart of home canned tomato.
  8. You need 1 can of diced tomatoes for chili.
  9. Prepare 2 Tbs of Worcestershire.
  10. It's 1 tsp of oregano.
  11. It's 1 tsp of paprika.
  12. Prepare 4-5 of fresh basil leaves chopped.
  13. Prepare 8 ounces of elbow macaroni.
  14. You need to taste of Salt/red pepper flakes.
  15. It's 2 Tbs of olive oil.

Goulash instructions

  1. Brown ground beef, drain and reserve.
  2. In 2 Tbs olive oil sautee red pepper flakes, chopped peppers first then onion until soft.
  3. Add all tomatoes and paste, Worcestershire, garlic and other spice to vegetables and simmer until chopped tomatoes soften (about 1 hour).
  4. While boiling macaroni add beef to tomato mixture and simmer.
  5. When mac is cooked, add to rest stir and let sit 30 minutes before eating.
  6. Garnish with shredded cheese.
