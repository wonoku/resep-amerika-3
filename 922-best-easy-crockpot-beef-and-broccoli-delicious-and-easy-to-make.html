Ingredients of Easy crockpot beef and broccoli

  1. It's 2 1/2 c of Beef broth.
  2. Prepare 1 Tbs of minced garlic.
  3. Prepare 3/4 c of low sodium soy sauce.
  4. You need 1/2 c of brown sugar.
  5. Prepare 2 Tbs of sesame oil (pricey but 1 bottle makes this over and over).
  6. You need 2-3 lb of lean stew meat.
  7. You need 4 Tbs of corn starch.
  8. It's 1 bag of frozen broccoli.
  9. You need of White rice.

Easy crockpot beef and broccoli step by step

  1. In crockpot whisk together broth, garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil..
  2. Add stew meat, cook on low for 4-6 hours. (6-8 if, like me, you're putting the meat in frozen).
  3. Mix corn starch with 1 c liquid from crock pot. Stir mixture into beef..
  4. Add frozen broccoli on top. Cook another 30 minutes..
  5. Serve over prepared white rice. (Or low mein noodles).
