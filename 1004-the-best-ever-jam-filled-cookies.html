Ingredients of The best ever jam filled cookies!

  1. Prepare 450 grams of all-purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  3. Prepare 130 grams of icing sugar.
  4. You need 3 of egg yolks.
  5. You need 225 grams of unsalted butter.
  6. You need of Strawberry or raspberry jam.

The best ever jam filled cookies! instructions

  1. Beat softened butter and sugar until creamy..
  2. Add egg yolks one by one and vanilla extract. Combine..
  3. Lastly, add flour and mix just until combined..
  4. Wrap the pastry in film and refrigerate for an hour.
  5. Extend pastry until 3mm thick. Cut an even amount of cookies and cut a smaller shape in the center of half the cookies..
  6. Bake at 175°C for 7 minutes. Let cool..
  7. Make cookie sandwiches with one whole cookie, jam, and a cutout cookie..
