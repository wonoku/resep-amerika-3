Ingredients of Cauliflower Buffalo Wings (vegan)

  1. Prepare 1 head of cauliflower cut or broken into bite size pieces (2").
  2. You need 1 c. of panko bread crumbs.
  3. It's 1 c. of tempura flour (bot chien).
  4. You need 2 tbs of ground flaxseed.
  5. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of ground pepper.
  7. Prepare of HOT Sauce: optional.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of red pepper powder.
  9. Prepare 1 Tbsp of paprika.
  10. You need 1 tsp of ground pepper.
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp of sugar.
  12. You need 1 Tbsp of olive oil.
  13. Prepare 2 Tbsp of water mixed with 1 tsp agar agar or cornstarch.

Cauliflower Buffalo Wings (vegan) instructions

  1. Mix flour, flaxseed, salt , pepper and warm water to a pancake consistency in small bowl..
  2. Put panko into another bowl. Coat cauliflower in mixed batter, then roll it in panko crumbs, then fry or bake to brown..
  3. For HOT sauce: Combine all ingredients, then heat 15 seconds in microwave. Drip sauce over cauliflower or keep sauce separate for dipping..
