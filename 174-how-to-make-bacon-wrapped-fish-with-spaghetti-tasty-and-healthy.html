Ingredients of Bacon - wrapped fish with spaghetti

  1. You need 4 of large fillets of white fish (terakihi, snapper etc).
  2. Prepare 8 of bacon strips.
  3. Prepare 1 packet of spaghetti.
  4. Prepare 500 grams of spinach.
  5. You need 1 of yellow, one red, one green capsicum.

  7. You need 50 grams of capers.
  8. You need 2 of tomatoes.
  9. Prepare of Olive oil.
  10. It's of Cracked pepper.
  11. You need of Sea salt.
  12. You need of Five spice.
  13. It's of Crushed garlic.
  14. You need 150 grams of grated parmesan.

Bacon - wrapped fish with spaghetti step by step

  1. Finely dice onions.
  2. Dice capsicums.
  3. Slice tomatoes.
  4. In an oven tray, lay fish fillets out on bacon strips, and season with salt, pepper, five spice and olive oil. Wrap bacon around pieces..
  5. Heat oven to bake on 250 degrees (F).
  6. In a pan, fry onions and garlic in olive oil until tender, seasoned with salt and pepper.
  7. Put fish in to bake.
  8. Add capsicum to pan and fry with onions etc until soft. Turn off heat and covered until needed..
  9. Add oil and salt to a large pot of hot water. Heat until boiling and add spaghetti..
  10. When spaghetti is boiled, drain water, and turn fish in oven from Bake to Grill to finish off..
  11. Combine spaghetti, cooked onions, capers and capsicum, spinach and parmesan, and stir until spinach is soft..
  12. Serve bacon-wrapped fish on pasta!.
