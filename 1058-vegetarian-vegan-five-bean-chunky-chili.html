Vegetarian (Vegan) Five Bean Chunky Chili instructions

  1. Sautee yellow onions over medium high heat with grapeseed oil until brown and see through..
  2. Combine all other non optional ingredients into giant crockpot. Bring to boil, turn heat to low..
  3. Add sauteed onions to crock pot mixture. And chipotle peppers..
  4. Cook on low until vegetables are desired texture / consistency. Three to Four hours, stirring occasionally..
  5. Serve with rice, salad, chips, cornbread, or by itself..
  6. Garnish with cilantro, green onions, cheese, sour cream, salsa..
