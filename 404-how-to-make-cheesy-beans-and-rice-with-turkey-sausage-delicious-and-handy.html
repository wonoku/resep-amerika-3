Ingredients of Cheesy Beans and Rice with Turkey Sausage

  1. It's 1 packages of Turkey Polska Kielbasa.
  2. It's 1 cup of Unprepared Basmati Rice.
  3. It's 1 can of Black beans, washed and drained.
  4. Prepare 1 can of Corn with green and red peppers.
  5. Prepare 1 can of Campbell's Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of Half and half (approx. measurement).
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of olive oil (approx. measurement).
  8. It's 5 tbsp of Chili powder (approx. measurement).
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of Cumin.
  10. It's 1 dash of Cayenne Pepper.
  11. It's 1/2 tsp of Garlic Salt.
  12. You need 1/3 cup of Frozen Chopped Onion.
  13. You need 6 of drops Tobasco Sauce.
  14. You need 1 of bullion Vegetable Broth.

Cheesy Beans and Rice with Turkey Sausage step by step

  1. Prepare rice according to package instructions leaving out all optional ingredients and adding vegetable bullion..
  2. Cut turkey sausage into thick slices..
  3. Add 1/4 cup of olive oil and onions to a pan and turn on heat. When hot add turkey sausage and saute until almost done..
  4. Add black beans and cook for a few minutes until cooked..
  5. Add corn and peppers and cook a few more minutes until done..
  6. Add cooked rice, chili powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, garlic salt and mix well..
  7. In bowl mix cheese soup, half and half, and Tobasco sauce..
  8. Add cheese soup to rice mixture. Mix well. May need to add a little oil (1 tbsp) to help with mixing. Cook until done..
