Ingredients of Avocado, Spam and Mayonnaise Chilled Pasta

  1. You need 100 grams of Pasta.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of ○Avocado.
  3. You need 50 grams of ○Spam.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of ○Mayonnaise.
  5. Prepare 1 of ○Lemon juice.
  6. Prepare 2 of leaves Basil leaves (to garnish).
  7. Prepare 3 of leaves ●Basil leaves (minced).
  8. It's 1 tbsp of ●Olive oil.

Avocado, Spam and Mayonnaise Chilled Pasta instructions

  1. Dice the avocado and spam..
  2. Put the ○ ingredients into a bowl, mix together and chill..
  3. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Cook the noodles for 1-2 minutes longer than usual..
  4. While the pasta is boiling, mince the basil..
  5. Strain the pasta in a colander and chill in ice water. Then drain off the water..
  6. Put the spaghetti into a bowl and add the ● ingredients. Also add the ingredients from Step 2..
  7. Arrange the spaghetti, avocado, and spam on a plate. Lastly, top with basil leaves to finish..
