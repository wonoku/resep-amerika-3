Cauliflower fried rice step by step

  1. Combine 1 teaspoon sesame oil with eggs. Whisk. Set aside..
  2. Chop cauliflower into chunks and grate in box grater..
  3. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in large skillet in high heat. Add cauliflower, green onions and frozen mixed veggies. Stir fry 3-4 minutes until cauliflower is starting to get tender..
  4. Add garlic, salt and pepper. Cook for another minute..
  5. Push mixture to sides to create a bowel shape in middle of pan. Add egg mixture. Scramble eggs and when done mix together with other ingredients in pan..
