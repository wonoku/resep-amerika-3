Ingredients of Crockpot Hot Chicken Sandwiches

  1. Prepare 1 of large pack chicken breasts (boneless, skinless).
  2. Prepare 2 of onions, sliced.
  3. It's 1 tablespoon of garlic powder.
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 packet of Italian seasoning (salad dressing mix, dry packet).
  6. You need 1 can of cream of chicken soup.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of water.
  8. Prepare 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese.

Crockpot Hot Chicken Sandwiches instructions

  2. Place half of sliced onions in bottom of crockpot.....
  3. Place cleaned chicken breast inside your crockpot on top of onions.....
  4. Place the rest of your sliced onions on top of chicken breast......
  5. Sprinkle your garlic powder and black pepper over chicken breast.....
  6. In a bowl combine the cream of chicken soup with the packet of Italian dressing mix and the 1/2 cup of water....Mix well......
  7. Pour that mixture over your chicken breast......
