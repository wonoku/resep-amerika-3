Ingredients of Hawaiian inspired casserole

  2. You need 1-1/4 pound of bulk sausage.

  4. Prepare 2 cups of applesauce.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of baking powder.
  6. You need 3 cups of sharp cheddar cheese, shredded.
  7. You need 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of parsley.
  9. You need 1 of large onion chopped.

Hawaiian inspired casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit.
  2. Grate and shred the cheeses, chop the onion, reserve 1 cup cheddar cheese. Then set 2 cups shredded cheese, parmesan,onions, into a bowl. Drain pineapples and add. Add applesauce, break sausage up, add in stirring a little bit till all is added. Add baking powder and parsley..
  3. Add to an oven safe dish..
  4. Slice most of spam into strips. Form into a wheel on top.
  5. Add reserved cup of shredded cheese.
  6. Bake in oven 40-50 minutes, till done let sit 10 minutes serve I hope you enjoy!.
