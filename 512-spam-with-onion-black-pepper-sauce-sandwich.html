Ingredients of Spam With Onion Black Pepper Sauce Sandwich

  1. Prepare 2 of bread.
  2. It's 1 can of spam.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of black pepper sauce(store bought).
  4. Prepare 2 of onion.
  5. You need 2 slice of Swiss cheese.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of Of butter.

Spam With Onion Black Pepper Sauce Sandwich instructions

  1. Butter the bread and toast then set aside.
  2. Slice the spam and bake at 200C for 8 minutes or longer depending the thickness.
  3. With 1 tbsp Of oil sauté the onion for 2 minutes then add in the black pepper sauce and mix well for another 1 minutes.
  4. Serve the bread with cheese and spam and generously of onion black pepper sauce.
  5. Open face spam With onion black pepper sauce and Swiss cheese.
