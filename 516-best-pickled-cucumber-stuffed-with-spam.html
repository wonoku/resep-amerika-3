Ingredients of Pickled Cucumber stuffed with Spam

  1. You need 4 ounces of spam.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of large English Cucumber.
  3. Prepare 1 of Mellon baller double sided.
  4. You need 1 inch of ginger root peeled and sliced.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of kosher salt.
  6. You need 2 tablespoons of juniper berries.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric.
  8. You need 1 cup of water.
  9. It's 3 cups of distilled white vinegar.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar.
  11. You need 1 tablespoon of yellow mustard seeds.
  12. You need 46 ounce of sterilized jar.

Pickled Cucumber stuffed with Spam step by step

  1. Add all the ingredients except the spam and Cucumber to the jar cover and shake..
  2. Cut the Cucumber into 1-1/4 pieces. Then scoop out the center..
  3. Scoop out balls of spam and add to the hollow part of the Cucumber repeat till done..
  4. Scoop out extra spam balls. Add everything including the scooped Parts of Cucumber to the jar.
  5. Set into the refrigerator for 2 weeks and then enjoy!.
