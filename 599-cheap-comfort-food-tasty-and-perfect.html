Cheap Comfort Food instructions

  2. Cut spam, garlic, onion, bell pepper and set aside..
  3. Heat skillet at med./high heat. Add oil let it get hot for a min. Add garlic but be careful not to burn it. Then add spam and brown, season to your taste..
  4. Add onions and bell peppers mix well and season to taste. Last add potatoes and have it cooking for about 5min..
  5. Enjoy!.
