Ingredients of One pan breakfast hash

  1. Prepare 2 of large russet potatoes.
  2. You need 1 of small onion.
  3. It's 8 oz of kielbasa, I used turkey kielbasa.
  4. You need 8 of large eggs.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheese, I used sharp cheddar.
  7. It's of Salt & pepper.
  8. It's 2 tablespoons of oil, I used grapeseed oil.

One pan breakfast hash instructions

  1. Finely dice the potatoes and onion. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat with the oil. Fry the potatoes and onions for 5-10 minutes or until softened and browned nicely. Season as desired with salt and pepper..
  2. Add the diced kielbasa and continue cooking a few minutes more..
  3. Whisk together the eggs with milk and salt & pepper. Mix in cheese. Pour eggs in pan and let set for a couple minutes before gently folding. Continue cooking and gently folding until eggs are cooked..
