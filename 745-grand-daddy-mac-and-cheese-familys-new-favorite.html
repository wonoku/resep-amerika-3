Ingredients of Grand daddy mac and cheese

  1. It's 2 of shallots, peeled.
  2. It's 3 of garlic cloves, peeled.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of olive oil.
  4. Prepare 4 of bacon slices, cooked, diced, reserve fat.
  5. You need 2 T of flour.
  6. It's 4 C of heavy cream (2C heavy cream, 2C half&half).
  7. It's 1 tsp of fresh thyme, chopped.
  8. It's 1 C of pepper jack cheese, shredded.
  9. Prepare 2 C of sharp cheddar cheese, shredded.
  10. It's to taste of S&P.
  11. It's 1 of # penne pasta, cooked.
  12. You need 1/2 C of Panko bread crumbs.
  13. Prepare 2 T of butter, melted.
  14. It's 2 T of fresh parsley, chopped.

Grand daddy mac and cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Place shallots and garlic in a small aluminum foil pouch and drizzle with olive oil. Roast 30 minutes until tender. Remove from foil and chop. In a large saute pan, reheat the reserved bacon fat over medium heat. Add the roasted shallots and garlic and saute for 1 minute. Add the flour and whisk for 1 minute. Whisk in the heavy cream and thyme. Reduce by a third. Stir in the cheeses until melted, creamy and thickened. Season to taste with S&P..
  2. Remove from heat and gently stir in the pasta..
  3. Pour into a 9 X 13 casserole that is sprayed with cooking spray..
  4. In a small bowl, mix together diced bacon, breadcrumbs, butter and parsley. Top mac and cheese with Panko mixture and bake uncovered at same heat until bubbling and lightly browned on top, 20-25 minutes..
