Ingredients of Russian Style Fried Potatoes

  1. You need 3 medium of russet potatoes.
  2. Prepare 1 of oil Peanut or Corn.
  3. You need 1 of salt.
  4. It's 1 of pepper.

Russian Style Fried Potatoes step by step

  1. Peal and wash potatoes.
  2. Cut in half long way, then again in half. To where you have thick Wedges.
  3. On your cutting board Sprinkle Wedges with salt and pepper, toss.
  4. Pour oil in skillet. Enough to fill bottom of pan 1 1/2inches. Turn it up a little below Med high heat. When oils hot put potatoes in. Oil should cover about half way up potatoes..
  5. Leave potatoes to get golden brown. Flip on each side till all sides are golden brown. You may need to adjust heat because all pans are different.
  6. Turn up higher at the end to get extra crispy. Take out and place on paper towels.
  7. Sprinkle with salt and serve right away.
