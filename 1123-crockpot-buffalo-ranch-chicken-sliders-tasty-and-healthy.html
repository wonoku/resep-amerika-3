Crockpot buffalo ranch chicken sliders step by step

  1. (Optional) Spray crock pot with cooking spray or use crockpot Liners. Place chicken in crockpot and season with the dry ranch.
  2. Mix wing sauce with butter and garlic and pour over chicken.
  3. Cook on low for about 8 hours.
  4. Shread chicken and mix up and serve on split Hawaiian rolls. Top sandwiches with a little red onion, some blue cheese crumbles and drizzle on additional wing sauce or ranch dressing if desired !.
