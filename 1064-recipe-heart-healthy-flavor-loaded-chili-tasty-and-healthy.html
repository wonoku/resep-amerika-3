Ingredients of Heart-healthy, flavor-loaded Chili

  1. You need 1 lb of 93/7 lean ground beef.
  2. It's 1 of large white onion, diced.
  3. You need 1 of medium green pepper, diced.
  4. Prepare 1 can of chicken broth.
  5. It's 1 can (14.5 oz) of diced tomatoes, undrained.
  6. Prepare 1 can (8 oz) of tomato sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 can of chili or kidney beans, undrained.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of cumin.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of chili powder.
  11. You need 1 tbsp of paprika.
  12. You need 1 tbsp of ground coriander.
  13. Prepare 2 tbsp of worchestershire sauce.
  14. Prepare 2 tsp of basil.
  15. You need 1 of *optional* shredded cheese for topping.

Heart-healthy, flavor-loaded Chili instructions

  1. Get your crockpot ready!.
  2. Chop your onion and green pepper. Brown ground beef and add chopped veggies and garlic. Save some onion to throw in the crockpot for a fresh crunch.
  3. Transfer your beef mixture to a crockpot, then add all other ingredients and stir together.
  4. Put your crockpot on low 6-8 hrs or high 4 hours and let the flavors marry! Top with shredded cheese and onion if desired..
