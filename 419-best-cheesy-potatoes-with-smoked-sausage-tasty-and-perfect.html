Cheesy potatoes with smoked sausage step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. boil water to boiling and add potatoes until just about done.
  3. While potatoes are cookin start cheese sauce by adding all remaining ingredients (except shredded cheese and paprika) into a saucepan until melted..use wire whisk and whisk often so it dsnt scorch.
  4. Fry sausage in skillet just until brown add onion and let simmer until onions are soft, remove from skillet.
  5. Drain potatoes and let chill once done then cube them.
  6. Add potatoes to skillet season with salt and pepper add meat back to pan and lightly toss.
  7. Take off the stove and transfer to a greased casserole dish.
  8. Pour cheese mixture over potatoes and sausage.
  9. Sprinkle cheese on top and sprinkle paprika on top..
  10. Put in oven for 32-35 mins and Voila'!! there u hv it.
