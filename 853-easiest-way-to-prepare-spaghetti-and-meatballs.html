Spaghetti and Meatballs step by step

  1. For meatballs, add beef, sausage, panko, breadcrumbs, egg, Parmesan, basil, salt and pepper..
  2. In a pan add oil and add the diced onion and garlic, let loose for 1:30. Add to meatballs. Mix meatballs..
  3. For sauce, use the other half of the Spanish onion and garlic, grate carrot and add canned whole tomatoes, and sugar, simmer in the pan until it becomes a sauce consistency..
  4. Put water on the boil and add salt. The water should taste like the ocean. Add spaghetti and immediately. Cook until al dente. Drain pasta and assemble..
