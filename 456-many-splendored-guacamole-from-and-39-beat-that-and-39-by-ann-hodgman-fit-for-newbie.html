Ingredients of Many Splendored Guacamole from 'Beat That' by Ann Hodgman

  1. You need 4 of ripe Hass avocados.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of chopped scallions / spring onions - green part.
  4. It's 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped.
  5. Prepare 2 of jalepenos, deseeded and finely chopped.
  6. It's 1 of tomato, deseeded and chopped.
  7. It's 1 tbsp of sour cream.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  10. It's 1 tbsp of white wine vinegar.
  11. It's 2 tsp of ground cumin.
  12. You need 2 tsp of chilli powder.
  13. You need 1/4 cup (65 g) of salsa.
  14. Prepare 1/4 cup (30 g) of Monteray Jack cheese, grated / shredded.
  15. It's 1/4 cup (35 g) of finely chopped chorizo / kielbasa sausage.
  16. It's 2 tbsp of tequila.
  17. You need 2 tbsp of freshly chopped cilantro / coriander.
  18. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.

Many Splendored Guacamole from 'Beat That' by Ann Hodgman step by step

  1. Roughly chop the avocados in a large bowl then mash half of the chunks.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients in order, stirring lightly after each addition.
  3. Serves around 5 cups / 10 people.
  4. This is seriously delicious! I wish I'd written it!! Credit to Ann Hodgman.
  5. To make this safe for Jack I used Violife vegan cheese in place of the Monteray and Alpro coconut yogurt in place of the soured cream.
