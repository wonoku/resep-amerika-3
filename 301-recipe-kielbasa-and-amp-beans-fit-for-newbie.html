Kielbasa & Beans step by step

  1. Cut kielbasa into desired shapes and sizes. Toss and pan fry with peppers and onions, oil, and seasoning salt until meat begins to brown and veggies soften..
  2. Meanwhile start the beans in a pot on medium heat and add in the brown sugar..
  3. When the meat is finished drain the grease and add to the beans. Mix well. Enjoy!.
