Ingredients of Cauliflower soup

  1. You need 1 head of cauliflower.
  2. You need 4 of each chicken bouillon cubes.
  3. It's 3 tbsp of cream of wheat.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of butter or margarine.
  5. You need 1 packages of kielbasa or beef smoked sausage sliced (optional).
  6. It's 7 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to taste (optional).
