Ingredients of Fried rice with cauliflower

  1. Prepare 2 cups of cooked rice.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of palm oil or any available cooking oil.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of cauliflower - florets.
  4. Prepare of small amount of fried egg- cut in flakes.
  5. Prepare of small amount of ham or chicken or fish fillet(choose any).
  6. It's 1 tsp of ground pepper.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of seasoning(msg, Knor or any of your choice).
  8. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  9. Prepare of minced garlic.
  10. Prepare of Optional only: saffron or turmeric powder as additional spices.
  11. Prepare of Optional: green peas.
  12. It's of Optional: fried nuts(cashew, almond, peanut) raisin for toppings.
  13. It's of TOPPING: crispy fried onion.

Fried rice with cauliflower instructions

  1. In a large pan, stir fry egg and cut in flakes.
  2. Stir fry ham, chicken, bacon or fish flakes- (choose any of these) and put in a separate plate together with the egg. Using the same pan & oil, fry the cauliflower until brown..
  3. Using the same pan, add oil if necessary then put the cooked ingredients and rice. Add seasoning & spices. Add green peas if available(optional) and stir constantly until the ingredients mixed well with the rice. Cook for few minutes..
  4. Put the fried rice in a plate and add topping. To make crispy fried onion topping: slice medium white onion into strips(thinly cut) and stir fry in oil over medium heat. Wait until the onion strips become brown & crisp. Sprinkle the crispy fried onion on top of the fried rice, add fried nuts or black raisin if available..
