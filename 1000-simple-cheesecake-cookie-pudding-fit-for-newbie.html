Simple Cheesecake Cookie Pudding instructions

  1. In a 8x8 dish, layer some graham crackers and vanilla wafers.
  2. Mix the softened cream cheese with the can of sweetened condensed milk in a bowl, until smooth..
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the puddings with the whole milk/liquid creamer, and then add the cream cheese mixture to the pudding mixture, and mix well until smooth..
  4. Then add a layer of pudding over the cookies. Then repeat again with layering cookies, and pudding mixture.
  5. Then top it with the cool whip mixture and then a mixture of crushed cookies sprinkled over it..
  6. Then put in the fridge for a few hours, or overnight until the cookies soften. I like my cookies very soft, so i leave in fridge overnight. But if you like your cookies with crunch, just put in fridge for a couple of hours to set..
