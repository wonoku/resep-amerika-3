Ingredients of Macaroni and Cheese

  1. You need 1/2 cup of butter.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of milk.
  6. It's 2-8 oz of packages of shredded sharp Cheddar cheese.
  7. You need 1 package (8 ounces) of elbow macaroni, cooked.

Macaroni and Cheese instructions

  1. Place 1/2 a cup of butter or margarine in a large microwave safe bowl and microwave on high about 1 minute..
  2. Stir in flour, salt and pepper. Mix well..
  3. Place milk in a 2 cup glass measuring glass and micro on high for 2 minutes..
  4. Gradually stir into flour mixture. Blend until smooth. Slowly stir in 3/4 or so of the cheese leaving extra for the top to be covered..
  5. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and micro on high for 2 minutes..
  6. Add macaroni and toss thoroughly. Transfer to 2 quart glass or ceramic casserole dish..
  7. Top with remaining cheese. Make sure the top is covered..
  8. Bake at 350 F° for 25 minutes..
  9. Broil on high for 2-5 minutes, to melt the cheese. Just watch carefully until it is crispy but not burnt..
