Ingredients of Easy Slow Cooker Chili

  1. It's 4 lb of Lean Chop Meat.
  2. Prepare 3 of Big cans of Crushed Tomatos.
  3. It's 1 of Big can of Red Kidney Beans (Drained and Washed).
  4. It's 6 of Medium cloves garlic minced (More or Less to your taste).
  5. Prepare 1 of Big yellow onion diced (More or Less to your taste).
  6. It's 2-3 tsp of Ground Cumin to taste.
  7. Prepare 2-3 tsp of Chili Powder to taste.
  8. Prepare of Salt and Pepper.
  9. You need of Oil.

Easy Slow Cooker Chili step by step

  1. Heat some Oil in a deep pan. Put onions and garlic in to brown..
  2. Add chopped meat to the browned onions. As the meat browns add chili powder, cumin, salt & pepper to taste. Let it cook completely..
  3. Add the meat mixture to the crock pot. Add approximately 3 cans of crushed tomatoes. Enough to cover the meat.   You can add your beans now or an hour before it's done. Cook high 4 hours..
