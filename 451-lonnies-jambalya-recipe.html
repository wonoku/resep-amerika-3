Ingredients of Lonnie`s Jambalya Recipe

  1. It's 1/2 of Rotisserie Chicken or chicken breasts shredded.
  2. Prepare 2 lb of kielbasa, smoked sausage or andouille sausage, cut into bite size pieces.
  3. It's 2 of Green Bell pepper.
  4. You need 5 of celery stalks diced.
  5. You need 1 of large Sweet onion diced.
  6. Prepare 2 (15 oz) of cans of Diced Tomatoes.
  7. It's 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  8. You need 2 tbsp of butter.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of minced garlic.
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp of Old Bay Seasoning or cajun seasoning.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of paprika.
  12. Prepare 2 dash of hot sauce.
  13. It's 3 cup of uncooked white rice.
  14. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  15. It's 10 cup of chicken stock.

Lonnie`s Jambalya Recipe instructions

  1. Add chicken stock and diced Tomatoes to crockpot on high or medium low in a stock pot.
  2. Heat oil & butter in a large pot over medium high heat. Saute sausage until lightly browned, about 8 minutes..
  3. Remove sausage and drain.
  4. Add onion, bell pepper, celery and garlic and saut√© until translucent..
  5. Remove vegetables then add the vegetables, sausage, shredded chicken to the crockpot or stock pot.
  6. Add old bay seasoning, hot sauce, salt and pepper.
  7. Note: this is a mild version of Jambalaya feel free to adjust the heat to your taste..
  8. Cook on high for 3 to 4 hours or on low for 6 to 8 hours in a crockpot or 2 to 3 hours on low in a stock potafter the Jambalya starts to boil..
  9. 45 minutes before you plan to eat, cook the rice as directed on the package..
  10. Serve Jambalya over rice.
  11. Note: This recipe takes about 45 minutes to prepare and a couple of hours or more to simmer before it's ready to eat.
