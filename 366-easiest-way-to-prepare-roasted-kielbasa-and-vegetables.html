Ingredients of Roasted kielbasa and vegetables

  1. You need 3 each of med sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces.
  2. It's 1 each of med sweet onion, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  3. You need 4 each of medium carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. It's 1 lb of smoked kielbasa or polish sausage, halved and cut into 1 inch pieces( I used about a pound and a half ).
  6. It's 1 each of med yellow summer squash, cut into 1 in pieces.
  7. Prepare 1 each of medium zuccini, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  8. You need 1/4 of salt or seasoning salt.
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp of pepper.
  10. Prepare 3 each of small to med tomatoes( I added these, not origionally called for in recipe).
  11. Prepare 1 of a touch of Dijon mustard, optional ( I didnt use it).
  12. It's 1/4 tsp of celery salt( optional, I added this ).

Roasted kielbasa and vegetables instructions

  1. preheat oven to 400°F. divide sweet potatoes, onion and carrots between 2 greased 15x10x1 inch baking pans. drizzle with oil; toss to cost. roast 25 minutes, stirring occasionally..
  2. add kielbasa or sausage, squash, zucchini, and tomatoes to pans ; sprinkle with salts and pepper. roast another 15 ~ to 20 minutes longer or until vegetables are tender. transfer to a serving bowl; toss to combine. if desired serve with mustard..
