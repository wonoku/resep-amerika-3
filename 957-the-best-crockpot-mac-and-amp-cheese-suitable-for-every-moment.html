Ingredients of The best crockpot mac&cheese

  1. It's 16 oz of Colby jack cheese.
  2. Prepare 16 oz of Monterey jack cheese.
  3. You need 16 oz of Velveeta original cheese.
  4. You need 16 oz of cooked elbow noodles.
  5. Prepare 1 stick of butter.
  6. It's 16 oz of milk.
  7. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper, to taste.

The best crockpot mac&cheese step by step

  1. Cook elbow noodles. Set to the side..
  2. Cube all cheeses and butter. Put to the side..
  3. Spray crock pot with non-stick cooking spray, I use PAM..
  4. Now, start with 1/3 of the noodles on the bottom of the crockpot and add cheese and butter on top. Do this continously, noodles will be last on top. Add milk. Add salt and pepper once finished with layers. I also added paprika for some color..
  5. Cook on low for 4 hours. Mix half way through time. Add more salt and pepper, if needed. You can add more but, you cannot take away so be careful..
