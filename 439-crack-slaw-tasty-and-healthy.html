Crack Slaw instructions

  1. Cook bacon until crispy on medium-high heat and then remove from pot. Sauté onions and carrots (and celery if you have it) in the rendered bacon fat..
  2. Add sausage (and any other veg you want). Then add coleslaw mix in small bunches, adding more as it wilts, until all of it is in the pot. Keep cooking until cabbage is cooked to desired tenderness..
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste and then serve and enjoy! :).
