Zucchini spaghetti with hickory smoked spam step by step

  1. I used this utensil to make the zucchini noodles I paid $11 us dollars at a flea market..
  2. After you make the noodles set aside.
  3. Take the spam out of the can.
  4. Heat oil in the pan. Slice the spam thinly. I got 9 pieces.
  5. Fry the spam in pan.
  6. Get them brown.
  7. When crispy move to some paper towels.
  8. In the leftover oil and drippings add the onion slices.
  9. Get them caramelized.
  10. Add tomatoes.
  11. Stirring constantly let all the bits on bottom of pan get incorporated into the sauce, its called deglazing the pan. Add tomato paste, sugar, salt, basil, parsley, and let thicken..
  12. The sauce should be very thick. Add the zucchini mix well. The zucchini will release its juices and thin the sauce out just a bit..
  13. Add cheese. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
