Ingredients of Meatballs and Misua (Almondigas) Soup

  1. Prepare 5 cloves of garlic, chopped.
  2. You need 1/2 of onion, chopped.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of cooking oil.
  4. It's 15 pcs of frozen meatballs.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of frozen mixed vegetables.
  6. It's 1 of Beef boullion.
  7. Prepare 4 1/2 cups of water.
  8. It's 1 cup of misua.
  9. Prepare 1 cup of greens, chopped.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of mushrooms, sliced.
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp of malunggay flakes.
  12. It's to taste of Salt and pepper,.

Meatballs and Misua (Almondigas) Soup instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Saute garlic and onions until fragrant..
  2. Add frozen meatballs and cook for 5 minutes. Add frozen veggies and cook for another 5 minutes..
  3. Add water and let boil. Add boullion cube..
  4. Add mushrooms and misua. Cook for 5 minutes..
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. Then greens and malunggay. Simmer for another 5 minutes and turn off heat. Serve hot..
