Ingredients of Veggie Roll

  1. You need 100 g of sheeps cottage cheese.
  2. It's 100 g of mixed frozen veggies.
  3. It's 5 tbsp of horse radish.
  4. You need 1 of egg yolk.
  5. Prepare Pinch of salt.
  6. It's 1 pack of puff pastry.

Veggie Roll instructions

  1. Cook the mixed frozen veggies in salted water till ready.
  2. Open the puff pastry and in middle section ditribute the sheeps cottage cheese and horse radish.
  3. Cut the puff pastry in such design as shown below.
  4. Ditribute the veggies equally.
  5. Fold it.
  6. Distribute one egg yolk on top.
  7. Bake in electric oven on 250 degree celsius or gas 5th setting till top is golden.
