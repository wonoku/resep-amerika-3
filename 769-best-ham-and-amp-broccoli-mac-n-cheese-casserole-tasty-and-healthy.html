Ham & Broccoli Mac N Cheese Casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Boil macaroni in water with a touch of oil and a sprinkle of salt..
  2. When macaroni is within 2 to 3 minutes of being cooked, add in the broccoli..
  3. Drain under running cool water in strainer; Set aside..
  4. Melt butter in large pot on medium low heat..
  5. Stir in flour until paste forms..
  6. Whisk in the milk and cream until chunks are dissolved..
  7. Add in all seasonings and continue to whisk until thickened; about 2 to 3 minutes..
  8. Add in the cheeses..
  9. Stir until smooth..
  10. Fold in the pasta, broccoli and ham..
  11. Add to a greased 9x13 baking dish..
  12. In a small bowl combine the melted butter, bread crumbs and parmesan cheese for topping..
  13. Place evenly on top of casserole..
  14. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown..
  15. Enjoy!.
