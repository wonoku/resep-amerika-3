Pan's mix (vegetarian option) step by step

  1. Chop the cabbage, onions, potatoes, spam, omit the spam if you want vegetarian version.
  2. In a pan add olive oil and whole cumin seeds.
  3. When the seeds start crackling add veggies and spam, if your using, add you spices let cook 5-7 minutes covered.
  4. Mix the sauce add to the pan mix with the veggies.
  5. Mix the sake and eggs, eat well add to the veggies.
  6. After eggs are done and incorporated into the veggies add peas stir well serve.
