Saute Tempeh (soy bean cake) instructions

  1. Fry tempeh until golden brown and crips then set it aside.
  2. In another pan add in 3 tbs oil and preheat the pan on medium high heat.
  3. Add in onion and cook until start browning then add in garlic and chilies and lower the heat to medium, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add fried tempeh and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add in sweetened soy sauce and honey and stir well.
  6. Taste it, there must be sweet, savory and a little spicy.
  7. If its done. Its ready to serve..
