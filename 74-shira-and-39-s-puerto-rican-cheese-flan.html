Ingredients of Shira's Puerto Rican cheese flan

  1. You need 1 can of Evaporated Milk.
  2. You need 1 can of Sweet Condensed Milk (regular size).
  3. Prepare 3 of Eggs.
  4. It's 1/8 tsp of Vanilla Extract.
  5. You need 1 box of 8oz cream cheese.
  6. You need 1 1/2 cup of Sugar.

Shira's Puerto Rican cheese flan step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 300°.
  2. In a blender, mix evaporated & condensed milk, eggs, vanilla extract and cream cheese. Mix well all the ingredients..
  3. In a sause pan, place the sugar and leave it set on low fire. Stir ocassionaly with a fork until sugar melts and makes a caramel. (WARNING: Do not touch the caramel with your hands. It will burn you.) Also, do not let the caramel boil. It will get burn and you might have to start over..
  4. Once you have the caramel done, remove from fire and pour it over a baking pan (not sure the size sorry), or cup cakes pans, and make sure caramel covers the bottom of the pan..
  5. Pour the milks mixture over the caramel, and place it in the oven for about 45 mins-1 hour or until you stick a tooth pick and comes out dry. Place it in the refrigerator for about 3 hours or overnight which is better..
  6. Enjoy !! :).
