Broccoli soup [keto] instructions

  1. Chop onions and broccoli. It doesnt have to be perfect. Because we are going to put it in a blender.
  2. Ghee and coconut oil in a big pan..
  3. Fry onions and than the broccoli. Add spices mix.
  4. Spice mix.
  5. Put water in the pan and cook this for 25 min. Than let it cool off for a one hour. Pour it in a blender..
  6. Fry in a pan the turkey salami and add the wipped cream and fresh coriander..
  7. Keep the soup in peanut butter jars. you need about 8 jars..
