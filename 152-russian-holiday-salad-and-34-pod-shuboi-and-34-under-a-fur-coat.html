Russian holiday salad "pod shuboi" (under a fur coat) step by step

  1. Wash vegetables. Do not peel. boil carrots and beetroots for 1-1.5hr, potatoes - 30min, egg - 20min.
  2. Peel vegetables.
  3. Cut herring to small peaces and remove bones.
  4. Prepare a deep bowl, mix onion and herring.
  5. Make a layer of onion and fish covering the bottom of the bowl.
  6. Add a thin layer of mayonnaise.
  7. Grind 1-1.5 carrots, spread evenly.
  8. Grind and spread 1 beetroot and then 2 medium potatoes.
  9. Cover with mayonnaise.
  10. You may do a second layer if bowl is deep enough: herring, mayonnaise, carrots, beetroot, potatoes, mayonnaise.
  11. Grind an egg on top.
  12. !! wash you grinder after beetroots so potatoes won't get colored.
