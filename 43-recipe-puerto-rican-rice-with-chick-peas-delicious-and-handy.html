Ingredients of Puerto Rican Rice with Chick Peas

  1. Prepare 2 cups of white rice (pre-cooked).
  2. It's 2 tablespoons of cooking oil.
  3. You need 8 ounces of diced ham.
  4. Prepare 1 can of chick peas.
  5. You need 1 can of Goya sofrito.
  6. You need 2 packets of Goya sazon.

Puerto Rican Rice with Chick Peas step by step

  1. Pour cooking oil in a deep frying pan and set to medium-high heat..
  2. Cook ham to desired doneness.
  3. Mix in chick peas, sofrito, and sazon..
  4. Reduce heat to low-medium heat and slowly pour in rice..
  5. Cook until heated through, stirring as needed to prevent burning. Add water if mix is too dry..
