Ingredients of Mexi style Cauliflower Rice

  1. Prepare 2 bags of frozen cauliflower rice (thawed).
  2. You need 1/2 of onion chopped fine.
  3. Prepare 1 of tomato chopped.
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons of chopped garlic.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of chopped hatch green chiles.
  6. It's 4 tablespoons of butter.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of cumin.
  8. Prepare 2 teaspoons of dried oregno.
  9. It's 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tablespoons of chile powder.
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon of paprika.

Mexi style Cauliflower Rice step by step

  1. Melt butter in pan..
  2. Sautee onion until translucent. Add garlic and spices and cook over medium heat until fragrant..
  3. Add cauliflower rice and green chiles and cook until rice is done and most of the moisture is evaporated. Taste for seasoning. I usually add Cajun seasoning instead of salt at the end..
