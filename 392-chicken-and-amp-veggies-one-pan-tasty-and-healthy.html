Chicken & Veggies One Pan step by step

  1. Pre heat oven 400°.
  2. Cut veggies into small pieces.
  3. Put veggies in a pan and the can of dieced tomatoes. Pour the olive oil over veggies. Sprinkle the seasonings over the veggies and then mix it all together..
  4. Cut up chicken and polska kielbasa into 1 inch pieces. Add them to pan and mix together with the veggies..
  5. Cover with foil. Leave edge vented. Cook for 45 minutes. Enjoy..
