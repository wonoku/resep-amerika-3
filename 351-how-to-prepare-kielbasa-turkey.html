Ingredients of Kielbasa ( turkey)

  1. Prepare 14 oz of kielbasa.
  2. You need 4 small of peppers chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of spicy mustard.
  5. It's 1 dash of salt & pepper.
  6. You need 1/2 of onion chopped.
  7. It's 3 clove of garlic chopped.
  8. Prepare 7 medium of potatoes cut in quarters.
  9. It's 1 can of cream of celery soup.

Kielbasa ( turkey) step by step

  1. in a large pot add garlic, onion, peppers, salt, pepper, mustard, and olive oil mix together. add cut potatoes mix again. put aside.
  2. in a large skillet brown the kielbasa..
  3. after browning mix in with other ingredients. let it marinate for at least an hr..
  4. cook over medium to low heat gradually add soup, or add full can of soup and bake until potatoes are tender.
