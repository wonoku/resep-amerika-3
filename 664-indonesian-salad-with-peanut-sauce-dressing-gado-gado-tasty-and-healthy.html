Ingredients of Indonesian Salad with Peanut Sauce Dressing (Gado-gado)

  1. You need of Vegetables (ex:carrot,lettuce,bean sprout,tomato,cucumber etc).
  2. It's of Boiled potato.
  3. Prepare of Rice cake (lontong).
  4. It's of Hard boiled egg.
  5. You need of Fried tofu.
  6. You need of Fried tempeh.
  7. You need of Tapioca crackers (krupuk).
  8. Prepare of Peanut Sauce Dressing.
  9. You need 6 of garlic cloves.
  10. Prepare 1 tbs of chili powder or paprika powder.
  11. You need 1-2 tsp of tamarind paste or to taste.
  12. You need 100 gr of coconut sugar.
  13. You need 2 tsp of salt.
  14. It's 2 tbs of sugar.
  15. You need to taste of Ground white pepper.
  16. Prepare 150 gr of natural peanut butter (no salt/sugar added).
  17. Prepare 500 ml of coconut milk.
  18. You need 100 ml of water.
  19. You need of Note :.
  20. You need of If you don't have peanut butter, use 200 gr ground fried peanut.
  21. It's of If you don't have coconut sugar, use granulated sugar.

Indonesian Salad with Peanut Sauce Dressing (Gado-gado) step by step

  1. Cut/slice the vegetables as you like. Set aside..
  2. Cut the remaining ingredients (potato, rice cake, egg, tofu, tempe) as you like..
  3. How to make Peanut Sauce Dressing : fry the garlic clove until golden brown..
  4. Put fried garlic, chili powder, peanut butter, coconut milk and water in the blender. Blend all together until smooth..
  5. Pour it into a sauce pan. Cook over medium heat..
  6. Add coconut sugar, salt and sugar..
  7. Stir until it bubbles. Add tamarind paste..
  8. Keep stirring it until it boils hard and looks oily. If it hasn't looked oily, keep stirring it and lower the heat down. Make sure to taste it and add sugar/salt/tamarind if needed..
  9. When the peanut sauce is ready, cool it down..
  10. How to serve : Put veggies and everything else, except the crackers, onto a serving plate. Drizzle the peanut sauce as much as you like ðŸ˜Š. Put the crackers on top. Enjoy. (The best way to eat it is to mix everything all together)..
