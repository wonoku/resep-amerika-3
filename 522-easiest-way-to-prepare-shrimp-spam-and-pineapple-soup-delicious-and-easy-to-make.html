Ingredients of Shrimp, Spam, and Pineapple Soup

  1. Prepare 1/4 cup of Heinz tomato ketchup.
  2. You need 1 1/3 lb of raw shrimp, you can use precooked.
  3. It's 20 oz of pineapple chunks in its juice.
  4. It's 12 oz of spam diced.
  5. It's 1 1/2 tbsp of tomato paste.
  6. You need 1 of salt on the pineapple only.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of honey.
  8. You need 2 tbsp of vegetable oil.
