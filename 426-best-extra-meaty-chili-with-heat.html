extra meaty chili with heat step by step

  1. grease your pot with oil.
  2. chop bacon into big chunks and brown then in your pot.
  3. then add chopped onion.
  4. add minced garlic.
  5. add sliced jalapeno.
  6. saute for 4-5 min.
  7. add grown beef and begin to brown.
  8. once brown slice your kielbasa and mix it in.
  9. now add your packets of McCormick to meats and stir.
  10. add both tomato sauce and diced tomatoes stir.
  11. now add all your left over beans.
  12. bring to a boil and simmer.
  13. add salt pepper pinch of sugar cayenne chili powder and garlic salt season to your liking.
  14. for extra heat add your favorite hot sauce.
