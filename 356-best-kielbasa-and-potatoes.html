Kielbasa and Potatoes step by step

  1. preheated oven to 400.
  2. clean potatoes and cut in cubes. toss with salt and pepper and olive oil. roast in oven for 50 mins..
  3. heat pan with olive oil and slice peppers, onion and mushrooms and garlic. add to pan and cook until peppers, mushrooms and onions caramelize. this can take 20 - 30 mind depending on the heat level, but so worth it..
  4. bring pot of water to boil, add kielbasa and cook for 7 mins. when done take out of water and cut into bite sizes when cool. set aside and cover..
  5. after potatoes cook for 50 mins, add pepper mixture and kielbasa and bake for another 20 mins or until to your liking..
