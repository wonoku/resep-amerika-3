Ingredients of Crockpot pulled pork chili

  1. You need 4 lb of pork roast-excess fat trimmed.
  2. It's 3 of garlic cloves,peeled.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of hot sauce.
  4. It's 3 tbsp of smoked paprika.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of chili powder.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of cumin.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp of cayenne powder.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of red pepper flakes.
  10. You need 1/2 tbsp of salt.
  11. Prepare 2 of yellow onions diced.
  12. Prepare 1 of green pepper,diced.
  13. You need 2 small of red peppers,diced.

Crockpot pulled pork chili step by step

  1. Add roast to crockpot,press knife in the spots and push garlic into the roast.
  2. Pour hot sauce over meat and sprinkle with all seasonings. Place veggies and tomatoes ontop of roast and seasonings..
  3. Cover and cook low 8-10hours.
  4. Shred pork when done cooking with two forks and remove bone.
