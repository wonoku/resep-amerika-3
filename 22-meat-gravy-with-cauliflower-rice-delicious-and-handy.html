Meat gravy with cauliflower rice step by step

  1. Heat a pan melt the margarine. Add water meat and dehydrated onions. Simmer break the meat up..
  2. Add salt, garlic, and pepper.
  3. Simmer till onions are rehydrated. Add broth bring to boil mix arrowroot powder and broth mix well add to boiling broth stirring constantly till thickened..
  4. Grate cauliflower, 4 cups, heat pan add margarine, cauliflower, salt, and oil..
  5. Cook 5-7 minutes. Let sit covered5 minuter serve put gravy over the top of cauliflower rice. Hope you enjoy!.
