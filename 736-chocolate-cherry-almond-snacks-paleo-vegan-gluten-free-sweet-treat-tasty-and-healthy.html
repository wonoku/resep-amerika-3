Chocolate, Cherry, Almond snacks (paleo, vegan, gluten free sweet treat) step by step

  1. melt chocolate chips (stovetop or microwave).
  2. drop multiple spoonfuls of chocolate onto a piece of wax paper.
  3. place a dried cherry in the center of each chocolate drop.
  4. place almonds circling the cherry in the chocolate drop.
  5. drizzle more chocolate on top if desired.
  6. put wax paper and treats in fridge for 10 minutes to set.
