Ingredients of Crockpot Spicy Barbecue Kielbasa

  1. Prepare 2 cup of Ketchup.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of Light brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of Creole mustard.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Hot sauce.
  6. It's 1 medium of onion, finely chopped.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of Bourbon.
  8. You need 2 lb of Kielbasa, cut into 1/2 inch rounds.
