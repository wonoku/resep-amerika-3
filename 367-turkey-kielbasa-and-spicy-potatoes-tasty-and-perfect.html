Turkey kielbasa and spicy potatoes instructions

  1. in one pan put the kielbasa in and enough water to cover and shake some soy sauce in water 3 should be enough then put on burner on med.and let it cook make sure to poke kielbasa with fork to allow soy sauce to seep into the meat.
