Ingredients of RACING RICE CAKE PASURUAN (Lontong Balap Pasuruan)

  1. Prepare 1 piece of tempeh, cut into 2x2 cm.
  2. You need 500 g of tofu, fried and cut to taste.
  3. You need 150 g of bean sprouts, discarded roots.
  4. Prepare 500 g of rice, for rice cake (lontong).
  5. Prepare of CHILI PASTE.
  6. You need 10 pcs of boiled cayenne pepper.
  7. Prepare 4 Tbsp of quality shrimp paste.
  8. You need 3 Tbsp of fried onions.
  9. You need 1 Tbsp of fried garlic.
  10. Prepare 50 ml of boiled water.
  11. Prepare to taste of Soy sauce.
  12. You need of DRESSING SAUCE.
  13. You need 1 stalk of lemongrass, crushed.
  14. Prepare 1 1/2 to 2 liters of water.
  15. You need 3 of lime leaves.
  16. Prepare 3 cm of galangal, crushed.
  17. Prepare 1 Tbsp of salt (to taste).
  18. You need 2 tsp of sugar.
  19. You need 2 Tbsp of oil for frying.
  20. You need of SPICES.
  21. Prepare 4 of garlic.
  22. Prepare 4 of onion.
  23. Prepare 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  24. Prepare 2 cm of turmeric.
  25. You need 4 of pecans, toasted.

RACING RICE CAKE PASURUAN (Lontong Balap Pasuruan) step by step

  1. Blend all ingredients seasoning and set aside..
  2. Blend all sauce ingredients, mix the soy sauce after the sauce was smooth. Add a little water so that it is not too thick, set aside..
  3. Heat oil and saut√© ground spices together with lemongrass, lime leaves and galangal until fragrant..
  4. Add the tempeh and water and stir well. Add the sugar and salt, and cook until cooked tempeh. Set aside..
  5. Put the cake in a serving dish, add the chunks of fried tofu..
  6. Pour sauce with tempeh on it, complete with chili paste. Serve warm..
