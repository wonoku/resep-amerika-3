A.l.s (avocado, lettuce and spam) sandwich instructions

  1. Slice spam and avocado into thin slices.
  2. Heat pan with oil inside on medium high.
  3. Once oil is hot carefully place the slices of spam and cook about 5 min per side. Then remove and allow to dry on paper towels..
  4. Toast bread in toaster.
  5. Assemble sandwich spreading mayo on toasted bread layering (in any order) two slices of spam, slice or two of lettuce and slices of avocado..
  6. Eat and enjoy.
