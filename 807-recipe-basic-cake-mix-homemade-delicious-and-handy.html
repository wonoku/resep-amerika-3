Ingredients of Basic Cake Mix, Homemade

  1. It's 7-1/2 cups of all-purpose flour.
  2. You need 2 tablespoons of baking powder, double acting.
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon of salt.
  4. You need 4-1/2 cups of sugar.
  5. You need 1-1/2 sticks of cold butter grated.

Basic Cake Mix, Homemade instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Mix the salt, sugar, baking powder, and flour..
  2. Grate the butter and fold into the flour mixture. Now grating the butter is not what we did in class, it's what we call now a hack. We used to cube and cut the shortening into the flour..
  3. Separate into 3 ziploc bags of equal measurements. Close to 3-2/3 cups each bag..
  4. Use or freeze in the ziploc bags. Each measurement makes a double layer cake 8 inches or one 9Ã—13 cake. I hope you enjoy!.
