Ingredients of THE BEST Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. It's 1 cup of vegan margarine (I use Becel Vegan).
  2. It's 1 cup of brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of white sugar.
  4. It's 2 of egg replacements.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla.
  6. Prepare 2 cup of whole wheat flour.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of oats.
  8. You need 1 tsp of baking soda.
  9. You need 1 tsp of baking powder.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of vegan chocolate chips.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of pecans or walnuts.
