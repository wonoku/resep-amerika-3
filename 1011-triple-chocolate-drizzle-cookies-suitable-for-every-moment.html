Ingredients of Triple Chocolate Drizzle Cookies

  1. It's 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of caster sugar (super fine sugar).
  3. You need 125 grams of butter, diced, at room temp.
  4. It's 1 of egg, beaten.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  6. It's 1 1/2 cup of self raising flour.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk chocolate baking chips.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of white chocolate baking chips.
  9. It's 3/4 block of of good quality chocolate.... (I use Cadbury).

Triple Chocolate Drizzle Cookies instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 160 c and prepare a baking tray lightly greased and lined with baking paper....
  2. In a mixing bowl cream together both sugars, butter and vanilla extract until pale and light..
  3. Add egg then fold in flour followed by the chocolate chips..
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked....
  5. Allow to cool completely on a rack...
  6. Meanwhile melt the block of chocolate in a heat proof bowl, placed over a pot of simmering water... (dont let any water get in the bowl) mix gently until melted and smooth..
  7. Once cookies are cool drizzle melted chocolate all over them!.
  8. Enjoy :).
